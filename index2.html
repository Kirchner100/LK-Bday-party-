The Occasion

Forever 29, continued

After skipping the big one last year, we're making up for lost time — properly, in the mountains. Pack your skis, your sunglasses, and your best après attitude.

Expect snow, champagne, long lunches, late nights, ski days, and a weekend full of favourite people. Crisp air, cosy dinners, dancing — and everyone who makes life worth celebrating.
